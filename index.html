The Trade-Offs: Pros & Cons

Why Invest in ETFs?

  • Diversification: Instantly reduces single-company risk.
  • Low Cost: Generally lower expense ratios and no sales loads.
  • Tax Efficiency: Unique structure minimizes taxable events.
  • Trading Flexibility: Buy and sell all day like a stock.
  • Transparency: Holdings are disclosed daily.
  • Accessibility: Easy access to niche markets and strategies.

What to Watch Out For

  • Market Risk: Diversification won't protect against a broad market decline.
  • Liquidity Risk: Niche ETFs can have wide spreads, increasing costs.
  • Tracking Error: The fund may not perfectly match its index's return.
  • Concentration Risk: Sector and thematic ETFs can lack broad diversification.
